ABOUT US

ABC learning Centre was established in 2001 with the sole and whole objective of reaching and maintaining the highest levels in the education sector. We have made giant strides and reached milestones surpassing even our expectations since then. ABC is recognized as the leading educational institution producing towering performance results in Sri Lanka.We cater to professionals on a ‘superlative professional level,’ we are proud to say that we are most sought after solution for professional education. Further we have a chain of international schools providing the Sri Lankan Students a comprehensive IT education in a well-disciplined environment.As the pioneer in the field of education, ABC is providing the best service to Sri Lankan Students.

Our Mission

Our mission is to search for and disseminate knowledge, promote learning, research and training to produce men and women proficient in their respective disciplines possessing practical skills and positive attitudes enabling them to contribute towards the manpower requirements of the nation. The institute will be a centre of excellence for research and development for Sri Lanka in general and the Sabaragamuwa Province in Sri Lanka

Our Vision

To be an internationally acclaimed centre of excellence in higher learning producing dynamic leaders and nation builders to guide the destiny of Sri Lanka

History

ABC was established in 2001. Currently, we offer both certificate. diploma and higher diploma courses and accommodate over 2000 students, including international students from various regions in the world. More than 1500 alumni have graduated from our faculties. We take great pride in producing graduates who make meaningful contributions to their communities and professions
