PRIVACY POLICY

Data Policy

ABC must protect restricted, confidential or sensitive data from loss to avoid reputation damage and to avoid adversely impacting our customers. The protection of data in scope is a critical business requirement, yet flexibility to access data and work effectively is also critical.

Open Educational Resource Policy

The ABC shall be committed to promoting the concept of OER and shall foster all efforts towards the adoption of OER in course design, development and delivery of ABC courses and other professional engagements and The OUSL shall develop capacity among its staff for effective adoption of OER thereby helping them to acquire necessary competencies and positive attitudes to sustain effectively in a knowledge society

Green Policy

The overarching goal of the Green Policy of the ABC learning center is to become one of the most environmentally sustainable universities in the island. This requires the transformation of the existing natural and built environment of the learning center to become environmentally friendly and gearing the teaching, research and advocacy functions conducted by the university to make Sri Lanka a sustainable country while promoting environmentally friendly lifestyles among the center staff and students.
