Carrots

Carrot is a root vegetable.The colour of the carrot is orange, red or yellow.It is a healthy vegetable.It contains many nutrients like protein, carbohydrates, fibres, and vitamins and minerals. A carrot with yellow and orange colour contains carotenoids and a carrot with purple and red contains anthocyanins.Many dishes are made up of carrot and are healthy like roasted carrots and carrot soups that will help you in improving your digestion system and also in weight loss.There are so many benefits of carrots like help in glow your skin and remove the dead skin cells. So, it is beneficial for a person to consume carrots in a day to stay fit and healthy.
